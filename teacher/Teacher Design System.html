Phase 4 · Teacher portal

Calm focused capability.

The Teacher portal is built for special-education staff managing 6–12 children through a structured day. It pairs capture-mode density in the classroom with synthesis-mode breathing room at the desk — and gives every child a stable ribbon color so the teacher always knows whose data they're looking at.

Accessibility safeguard

Ribbon color is supplementary identity, never the only identifying signal. Name + photo/initials + ribbon = redundant identification (text + image + color) — satisfies WCAG SC 1.4.1. All 12 ribbon colors pass AA contrast against white text in the avatar circle.

What ribbons are not

Decoration

Never on dashboards, empty states, or non-child cards.

Group cards

Don't ribbon a group, document, or admin-sourced message.

Color drift

A child's ribbon never changes — the recognition affordance depends on stability.

Parent portal

Parents only see one child — no disambiguation needed.
